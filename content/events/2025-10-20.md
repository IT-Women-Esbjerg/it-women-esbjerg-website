## Description

Preparing for your next career step? 🎯

Whether it’s an internship, a student job, or your first full-time role, join our hands-on interview workshop to gain the tools and confidence to shine in every interview!

💬 You’ll learn how to:

- Make a strong first impression and understand personality-based interviews.
- Navigate the Danish job market as an international candidate.
- Tackle real interview tasks and case challenges.
- Build a professional portfolio that showcases your skills.
- Practice with mock interviews and receive constructive feedback.

Join us for a practical, hands-on session that will help you sharpen your interview skills and take the next step in your career! 🥳
