## Description

Join our first IT Women Esbjerg meetup after summer!

We’re gathering on Thursday 18. September at 16:30 at EASV Esbjerg for a relaxed afternoon with snacks, sodas and good conversations.

This is a chance to (re)connect, meet new faces and get to know what IT Women Esbjerg is all about!

💬 What you can expect:

- Meet other female students and new professionals in IT/tech
- Hear more about our mission and community
- Learn about upcoming activities and opportunities
- Be part of shaping the future of IT Women Esbjerg

And as a little extra, we’ll reveal and talk about our new logo and visual identity – a fresh look for the new season. 💅

Whether you’re a student in IT/tech or just starting your career, you’re more than welcome!

Please let us know if you are attending by filling out https://lnkd.in/dg3Vxypw
