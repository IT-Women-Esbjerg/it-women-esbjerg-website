## Description

This month’s meetup is all about two important things: how you show up online — and pizza.

We’ll be gathering for an evening of easy conversation, good company, and some real talk about personal branding. Think of it as a mix of mini-reflections and spicy flash questions like:
✨ If someone Googled you right now — what would they see?
🦸 If your personal brand had a superhero name, what would it be?
🤔 Do employers actually check your LinkedIn before calling you in?

Whether you’re building your profile for a new job, promoting your freelance work, or just curious about how others manage their online presence — this is for you.

We’re ordering pizza for everyone, so please head over to the LinkedIn group to RSVP so we can plan accordingly. 🍄🌱🧀

Let’s eat, laugh, and maybe even update our LinkedIns afterward 😄
See you on the 25th!

## Recap

We explored personal branding in a refreshingly real way:

💬 Why authenticity matters — especially in job hunts, freelance work, and networking

🌟 How to highlight your unique talents and strengths across online platforms

💡 And we gave each other tailored suggestions for simple next steps to boost our personal brands

The energy? Supportive, curious, and fun. 🙌

Whether you walked away with a new LinkedIn headline, a confidence boost, or just a very full stomach — we hope it inspired you to take ownership of your digital story.

Huge thanks to everyone who showed up and helped make the night a success. 💛

Until next time!
