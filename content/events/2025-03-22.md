## Description

For this month, we would like to invite you all for a dinner/drinks event at Streetfood Esbjerg on the 22nd of March (Saturday). Whether you’re looking to make new connections, swap ideas, or just hang out with like-minded people, this is the place to be.

Everyone will be responsible for getting their own food and drinks, but the prices are affordable, and there are many options to choose from, so we are sure everyone will find something they’d like.

We will book a table so there is enough space for everyone. Let us know if you’re coming so we can plan accordingly!

## Recap

The best proof of a good meeting is the word of YOU – the fabulous IT women. That’s why we would like to share a story from one of us:

“I was about to give up on this meeting; I had an endless to-do list, and the day was busy. However, I decided to go into Streetfood with a racing heart, hoping someone would talk to me and I wouldn’t feel awkward.

What I didn’t expect was to learn more than just about tacos! I ended up having in-depth discussions on IT careers, work-life balance, and meeting women who just understood me. One of us even promised to introduce me to a mentor before I’d even taken my first bite of taco. I was amazed!

These meetups turned out to be far more transformative than I expected. No slides, no PowerPoints – just honest advice and recommendations from women who have walked in your shoes and are on your side.

What you can expect to take away:

1. Real encouragement from women sharing their insider knowledge.

2. Direct access and connection to opportunities.

3. An innovative approach to technology careers.

4. Confidence, seeing others succeed in your dream career path.

If you need a boost or some fresh ideas, these meetups are a hidden gem. You should definitely attend – you never know where it might take you!”

Will we see you at the next one? Don’t let fear have the last say – leave a quick “I’m in!” No pressure, just honest chat. 💬
