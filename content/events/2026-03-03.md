At the beginning of March, some of us will be attending the SheLeadsTech event in Kolding, and everyone is more than welcome to join, the more the merrier! 😊
[Get your free tickets here](https://www.businesskolding.dk/arrangementer/givetogain-she-leadstech-kolding#tilmelding) and join us for an inspiring afternoon of learning, networking, and collaboration.

---

### About SheLeadsTech

SheLeadsTech is an international initiative focused on empowering and promoting women in tech through inspiration, networking, and leadership – with the goal of creating a more diverse tech industry.

The event is open to everyone with an interest in women and technology, regardless of background or gender, and offers both inspiration and networking opportunities.
This evening features an inspiring speaker from the University of Southern Denmark (SDU).
